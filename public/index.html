COVI-DL

What is COVI-DL?

COVI-DL is a deep learning based web app for users to find out their chances of dying if they get Covid-19 (with 81% precision) based on their current pre-existing medical conditions. They can also find out whether or not they have Covid-19 (with 94% precision) based on their chest-xray.


The app uses an image of the patient's X-Ray to classify the user into one of 3 categories: Covid-19, Pneumonia, or Healthy and is trained and deployed using Google Cloud AutoML. The Survival Chance part of the app uses the patient's medical information to predict how likely they are to recover from Covid-19. Trained using a regression neural network model.




** Regression model is created from data gathered from Mexico and is reflective of their population's circumstances

How does it Work?

(Recommended for Everyone)

Find out your chances of surviving Covid-19 by entering in your medical information

(Recommended for Health Professionals)

Determine if patient has SARS-CoV-2 by uploading image of their chest x-ray
